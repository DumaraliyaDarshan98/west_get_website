FAQ’s - Cost and Pricing

  • 1.  What are the different subscription packages offered by Westgate, and what do they include?

    Westgate offers Basic, Silver, Gold, and Platinum subscription packages, each with varying levels of services and benefits.

  • 2. What is included in the Basic subscription package, and how much does it cost?

    The Basic package grants full access to project listings and supplier resources, including feasibility study services, for an annual fee of 149,999 INR.

  • 3. How does the Silver subscription package differ from the Basic package, and what is the cost?

    The Silver package includes bid review services by the UK team for up to 25 projects/year, priced at 399,999 INR annually.

  • 4. What is the Gold subscription package, and how is it priced?

    The Gold package offers on-demand bid review services by UK third-party experts, with upfront payment of 50% of the fee and the remaining 50% after winning the bid.

  • 5. How does the Platinum subscription package differ from the Gold package, and what is the pricing structure?

    The Platinum package provides on-demand bid writing services by UK third-party experts, with the same upfront payment and post-bid payment structure.

  • 6. What payment methods are accepted for subscription fees, and when are they due?

    Subscription fees can be paid via bank transfer, credit card, or other accepted methods, with payment due annually upon subscription renewal.

  • 7. How does Westgate ensure transparency in its pricing and billing practices?

    Westgate maintains transparency by clearly outlining subscription package details, fees, and payment terms in its pricing and billing information.

  • 8. Can Suppliers request a breakdown of fees or an invoice for their subscription package?

    Yes, Suppliers can request a breakdown of fees or an invoice for their subscription package upon request.
